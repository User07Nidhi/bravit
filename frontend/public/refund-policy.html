Refund / Return Policy

Goods return is a scheme provided by respective sellers directly under this policy, offering the options of exchange, replacement, and/or refund. Note that all products may not have the same return policy. For all products, the return/replacement policy on the product page shall prevail over the general return policy.

Return Policy Categories

Product Category Return Period
Supplements 5 days
Health & Hygiene Products 7 days

General Rules for a Successful Return

  • If the seller is unable to process a replacement for any reason, a refund will be provided.
  • During open box deliveries, if you receive a different or damaged product, you will be given a refund (on-the-spot refunds for cash-on-delivery orders).
    Note: Once you have accepted an open box delivery, no return request will be processed except for manufacturing defects.
  • In case of online order and payment, a video of opening the box is required as a supportive document for a successful return.
  • Any written complaint must be made within 3 days of product delivery to process a return.

Undelivered Product Issues

If the product was not delivered but you received a delivery confirmation email/SMS, report the issue within 7 days from the delivery confirmation date for investigation by the seller.
